# ISS-002: Critical and High Vulnerabilities Past Remediation SLA

## Rating
High

## Observation
Several critical and high vulnerability findings remained open beyond defined remediation timelines, including findings affecting critical and internet-facing assets.

## Risk
Delayed remediation of severe vulnerabilities increases exposure to exploitation, service disruption, and regulatory concern in a financial-services environment.

## Root Cause
Patch remediation governance and escalation pathways were not consistently effective for high-risk findings.

## Recommendation
Establish stronger SLA escalation for overdue critical and high findings, prioritize internet-facing and critical assets, and require retest evidence before closure.

## Management Action
Vulnerability Management leadership will prioritize overdue findings, escalate unresolved items, and track closure validation for all high-risk exceptions.
